My thoughts about the Philippines

People in the PH are very radiant. From them, you can always receive positive energy and a warm smile as you pass them by.

I have lived in the Philippines for 3 years (2018-2021). It is a beautiful place, especially if you visit the beaches. Even though I do not live there anymore, I still consider it my home since my family currently lives there.

One of my favorite places to visit with my family is Puerto Galera. Since we live far from the beach, we would visit this town once in a while.

Even though poverty rates are very high in this country, people still find joy in many different things, especially in singing karaoke, and dancing.

What I love the most about this place is the view. I love how the colors such as green and blue complement each other. I also love the underwater world where you can see different kinds of fish.
